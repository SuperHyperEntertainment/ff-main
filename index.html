What is StudioPro?

StudioPro is your centralized hub for animation production. It bridges the gap between directors, animators, lighters, and voice talent. Instead of losing track of files in chat logs, StudioPro gives you a clean, real-time dashboard to track scene progress, manage voice actor assignments, and monitor render farm analytics.

How to Use It

1

Secure Login

Use your studio-assigned credentials to access your personalized POV. Every role sees exactly what they need.

2

Check Assignments

Review your dashboard daily for new tasks, script updates, or scene rendering approvals from the Director.

3

Update Tracker

Keep the team in the loop by pushing scene statuses from "To Do" to "Rendering" to "Review."
